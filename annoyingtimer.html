The annoying timer

Have you ever wanted your browser to bother you every x minutes that it has been x minutes? Who hasn't? Well, now you can.

Introducing the productivity tool to end all productivity tools: the annoying timer. Now, you can have your work rudely interrupted by your browser reminding you that you did not get as much done as you planned.

Just input a number of minutes to begin.

Warning: once it starts, it won't stop. Ever. Unless you close the page, I guess.
